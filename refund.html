Cancellation Policy

Hamrolocale allows cancellation of orders anytime before dispatch up to 6 hours. If you wish to cancel an order, please contact us at info@hamrolocale.co.np as soon as possible. We will refund your payment in full if your cancellation request is received before dispatch.

Returns Policy: Due to the nature of food items we accept no returns. Only refunds maybe issued where applicable.

Refunds Policy: We take great pride in the quality of our food products and want our customers to be satisfied with their purchases. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please contact us at info@hamrolocale.co.np as soon as possible.
Complaints about the quality and taste of the food products will be handled on a case-by-case basis and at the discretion of Hamrolocale. We may offer a full or partial refund for such cases. Customers are requested to contact Hamrolocale at the earliest for better chances of a refund. We may ask for photographic evidence or request the return of the product in question to process the refund.

Refunds will be credited within 3 business days from the date of approval of the refund request. The refund will be credited back to the same account that was used to make the purchase.
Please note that shipping charges are non-refundable.

Exceptions: In certain cases, we may not be able to offer a refund or cancellation. These cases include, but are not limited to:

Orders that have already been dispatched or delivered
Orders that have been partially consumed
Orders where the customer has provided incorrect or incomplete shipping information

Changes to the Policy: Hamrolocale reserves the right to modify this refunds and cancellation policy at any time, without notice. Your continued use of our website after any modifications to the policy indicates your acceptance of the modified policy.
