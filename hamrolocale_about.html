Who We Are

2 Founders

Hamrolocale is a persistent journey of two entrepreneurs in a very unique segment of business.

Startup

Hamrolocale is a rural startup that took shape during the difficult phase of Covid-19 in 2020.

Hamrolocale

Hamrolocale is based in Pork, our village-town in Bardiya, and the prime focus is in growing with the community in Kathmandu, lalitpur, Bhaktapur.

Tradition Meats Innovation

While the concept of Hamrolocale had been brewing in our minds from some years back but Covid was definitely the catalyst that made us pre-execute our ideas into action.

Through our unique branding, packaging, innovation and social media marketing we were able to catch the imagination of our audience who very quickly turned into our customers. We have actively been involved in expanding our business since our inception in June, 2020 and through this journey we had come to realize that we can build our business while simultaneously growing with the farming community of Khowang and close by villages who provide us raw materials. Initially we were not directly involved with farmers from whom we needed raw materials in manufacturing our products. Today ‚Äì we are involved directly with over 3 person and indirectly with over 20 peoples. Bettering livelihoods with the growth of Hamrolocale has become one of our prime objectives now.

Hamrolocale success during the pandemic is a testament to the importance of innovation and adaptability in the face of challenges. By focusing on the needs of our customers and rapidly developing uniqueness to our products, Hamrolocale has been able to grow and thrive during a difficult time for businesses all Nepal.
